## Data Sources

| Source | Platform | What You Find |
|--------|----------|---------------|
| Google Ads Transparency Center | Google | Active ads, formats, geo targeting |
| Meta Ad Library | Meta/Instagram | All active ads, creative, copy, spend range |
| TikTok Creative Center | TikTok | Top ads by industry, trending creative |
| Google Auction Insights | Google | Impression share, overlap rate, outranking share |

## Competitive Analysis Framework

### 1. Ad Copy Analysis
- Headlines: primary messages and value propositions
- CTAs: what action they drive
- Offers: pricing, discounts, trials
- Tone: professional, casual, urgent, educational
- USPs: unique selling propositions
- Pain points they address

### 2. Creative Strategy
- Formats: image, video, carousel, UGC
- Visual style: photography vs illustration vs stock
- Creative volume (indicator of testing velocity)
- Refresh frequency

### 3. Messaging Theme Matrix

### 4. Keyword Intelligence (Google)
- Brand keyword bidding: competitors on your brand?
- Keyword overlap and gaps
- Match type strategy (estimated)

### 5. Spend Estimation
- Meta Ad Library shows spend ranges for political/social ads
- Google Auction Insights + impression share = directional estimate
- Formula: `Monthly Spend = Impressions x CPM / 1000`

## Gap Identification
- Platform gaps: where competitors are NOT
- Messaging gaps: pain points no one addresses
- Audience gaps: segments not targeted
- Creative gaps: formats not used

## When Competitors Bid on Your Brand
- Always run brand campaigns to defend
- Dynamic keyword insertion for prominence
- Ad copy emphasizing unique differentiators
- Consider bidding on competitor brand terms

## When You're Outspent
- Focus on efficiency (better targeting, creative, landing pages)
- Target long-tail keywords competitors ignore
- Use Exact match for precision
- Double down on retargeting (lower CPA)
