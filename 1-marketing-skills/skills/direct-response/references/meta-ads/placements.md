## Photo Ad Formats

Three aspect ratios available:
| Format | Aspect Ratio | Min Resolution | Max Resolution |
|--------|-------------|----------------|----------------|
| Square | 1:1 | 600x600 | 1936x1936 |
| Landscape | 1.91:1 | 600x315 | 1936x1936 |
| Vertical | 4:5 | 600x750 | 1936x1936 |

- Square is slightly larger in the Instagram feed
- Photos are fantastic especially for retargeting
- Most Instagram content is photos anyway -- fit your ads into what your market sees
- Photos usually get MORE reach than videos

### Pro Tip: Native Research
Go to likes on your ad > click through profiles > look at their followers > study what those people post. Get your photos to match what people are used to seeing. This makes ads feel native rather than obviously sponsored.

## Video Ad Formats

| Format | Use Case |
|--------|----------|
| Square (1:1) | Feed placement |
| Landscape (16:9) | Limited use |
| Vertical (9:16) | Stories, Reels -- takes up most attention |

- Max file size: 4GB
- Feed videos: up to 120 seconds
- Always use captions -- 80%+ of users watch without sound
- Vertical (9:16) takes up the most screen real estate
- Videos begin playing automatically -- no click needed

## Story Ads

- 15 seconds or less recommended
- Quick CTA: swipe up
- Cost usually much lower than feed placements
- More reach on stories than feed (people go through stories more)
- Stories are time-limited (24 hours) so people check them frequently
- Available on Instagram, Facebook, and Messenger

### Format Options
- Classic 15-second stories
- 45-second stories (3x 15-second clips)
- Long videos with "Keep Watching" button (auto-shows first 15 seconds)

> "Stories are most of the time where people spend the most of their time on Instagram because stories are limited to 24 hours."

### Image Size for Stories
- 1080x1920 (9:16 ratio)

## Carousel Ads

- Up to 10 images or videos per carousel
- Users swipe through cards
- Huge creative opportunity

### Best Uses:
1. **Testimonials** -- 10 different testimonials, swipe through
2. **Product features** -- one feature per card
3. **Story format** -- long landscape image chopped into pieces for swiping
4. **Book contents/chapters** -- for book funnel ads
5. **Before/after** sequences

### Setup
- Ad level > select "Carousel" under format
- Defaults to 3 cards, can add up to 10 with the + button
- Each card can be image or video
- Instagram: no headlines, only description text shows

## Slideshow Ads

- Similar to video but made from series of still images
- Plays as a video in the feed
- Can add text and audio
- Built-in editor in Ads Manager with stock library and fonts
- Available for some advertisers (not all accounts have it)
- Good option when no video content exists

## Caption Length

- Instagram ad captions: up to 2,200 characters
- Text appears below the image/video
- Instagram currently does not show headlines (unlike Facebook)
- Keep critical info above the "...more" fold

---

## Platform-Specific Creative Tips

### Instagram Feed
- Square or vertical format preferred
- Native-looking content > polished ads
- Must feel like organic content

### Instagram Stories
- Vertical (9:16) only
- 15 seconds or less ideal
- Swipe-up CTA
- Cheapest placement typically

### Facebook Feed
- Square or vertical
- Headlines show on Facebook (not Instagram)
- More text-tolerant than Instagram

### Reels
- Vertical (9:16)
- Fast-paced, authentic
- Can drive very cheap traffic
