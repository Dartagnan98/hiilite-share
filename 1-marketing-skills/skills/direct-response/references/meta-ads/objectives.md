# Types of Objectives
**Source: Jeremy Haynes / Megalodon Marketing -- Week 3 Lesson 20**

---

## Available Campaign Objectives

Results on Instagram/Facebook are based on the objectives you select when creating a campaign.

| Objective | Feed | Stories | Notes |
|-----------|------|---------|-------|
| Brand Awareness | Yes | Yes | Top-of-funnel awareness |
| Reach | Yes | Yes | Maximum impressions |
| Traffic | Yes | Yes | Drive clicks to URL |
| Engagement | Yes | Yes | Likes, comments, shares |
| App Installs | Yes | Yes | Mobile app downloads |
| Video Views | Yes | Yes | Optimize for ThruPlay (15 sec) |
| Lead Generation | Yes | Yes | In-platform lead forms |
| Messages | Yes | No (Feed only) | Messenger/WhatsApp conversations |
| Conversions | Yes | Yes | Optimize for specific actions |
| Catalog Sales | Yes | No (Feed only) | Dynamic product retargeting |
| Store Traffic | Yes | No (Feed only) | Drive to physical locations |

---

## When to Use Each Objective

### For Direct Response (Most Common)
- **Conversions** -- the go-to for lead gen and sales. Optimize for specific standard events (purchase, lead, complete registration)
- **Lead Generation** -- in-platform forms, good for mobile, lower friction
- **Messages** -- for conversational sales / chatbot funnels

### For Content Warming (Venus Fly Trap / Harvester)
- **Engagement** -- get likes, comments, shares on content pieces
- **Video Views** -- optimized for ThruPlay (someone reaches 15 seconds in a video)
- Use BOTH engagement and video view campaigns for same content -- algorithm reaches two different types of people based on their behavior

### For Retargeting
- **Conversions** -- retarget warm audiences with direct response
- **Catalog Sales** -- dynamic product retargeting for e-commerce

### For Scaling (CBO/Cyclic)
- **Conversions** with standard events -- always preferred over custom conversions
- Run cyclic campaigns optimizing for different funnel steps

---

## Objective Selection Rules

1. **For cold traffic warming:** Engagement + Video Views (dual campaign on same content)
2. **For direct response:** Conversions with standard event optimization
3. **For retargeting:** Conversions or Catalog Sales
4. **Never use Traffic objective** when you want conversions -- optimizes for clicks, not buyers
5. **Avoid Reach/Brand Awareness** unless specifically running awareness campaigns

---

## Standard Events for Conversion Optimization

When selecting Conversions objective, optimize for these standard events (not custom conversions):

- View Content
- Add to Cart
- Initiate Checkout
- Purchase
- Lead
- Submit Application
- Complete Registration
- Contact
- Schedule
- Subscribe
- Start Trial

**Why standard events > custom conversions:** All 7M+ advertisers' data vs only your pixel data. Thousands of times more data for the algorithm to work with.
