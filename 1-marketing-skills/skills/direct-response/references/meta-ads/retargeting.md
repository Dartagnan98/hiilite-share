**Source: Jeremy Haynes / Megalodon Marketing -- Week 3 Lesson 8 + Venus Fly Trap + Harvester SOPs**

## Core Principle

Social interaction is now retargetable on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn. Everyone who watches your videos, interacts with your pages/posts, messages you, or saves posts is retargetable.

This is the modern-day email list -- built inside social media channels for 100x-1000x cheaper.

- Average cost per video view/engagement: $0.001-$0.02
- Competition spending average $1.00 driving people to website
- Your dollar goes 100x-1000x further
- Open rates near 100% (you're in their newsfeed)

## Retargeting Audience Types

### Engagement-Based Audiences
Set up with different date ranges: 5 days, 10 days, every 5-day increment up to 365 days

- Facebook page interactions
- Instagram page interactions
- Video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%, 95% thresholds)
- Post engagers (likes, comments, shares, saves)
- Messenger conversations
- People who clicked on ads

### Website-Based Audiences
- All website visitors (30, 60, 90, 180 days)
- Specific page visitors
- Time-on-page audiences (using tools like ConnectRetarget)
- Thank you page visitors (buyers)

### Custom Audiences
- Email lists
- Phone number lists
- App activity
- Catalog interactions

## The Venus Fly Trap Strategy

### Purpose
Take a cold audience from not knowing you to having the right biases, beliefs, and objections handled BEFORE presenting offers.

### The Sequence

**Video 1 (Engagement + Video View Campaigns):**
- Establish authority bias, social proof, broad value
- NO call to actions whatsoever
- 1-3 minutes ideal
- Target cold audiences (same as you'd use for direct response)
- Goal: get them to 10 seconds (average time spent on a post = 1.8 seconds)

**Video 2 (Engagement + Video View):**
- Establish brand promise subliminally
- Make your biggest claim or boldest statement
- Plant and relate to deep buying motives
- Leverage curiosity bias -- be so valuable they wonder how they hadn't heard of you
- Main intention: establish your positioning

**Videos 3.1, 3.2, 3.3 (Engagement + Video View):**
- Test 3-5 different forms of "category content"
- The Rotisserie Chicken Analogy: people don't buy for one reason, just like a grocery store serves many buyer types
- 20+ deep buying motives can become hooks/angles for content
- Manage beliefs of potential buyers
- Minimum 3 categories

**Conversion Ads 1-3 (Now you pitch):**
- 3-5 variations with different hooks and angles
- Only shown to warmed-up audiences (people who went through videos 1-3)
- Ad formats for warm traffic:
  - 60-second video: 3-4 problems > product available > 3-4 results
  - 2-minute video: same structure with storytelling
  - 4 retargeting videos: deeper problem detail, call out actions they've taken
  - 2 fifteen-second vertical stories: quick, swipe-up CTA
  - 2 retargeting stories: remind them to take action
  - "You didn't buy, come back" video: direct call-out

**Videos 4-11 (Ongoing Content):**
- Maintain belief management and objection handling
- Keep content in front of warmed audience
- Prevents them from being conditioned to ignore your ads from over-pitching
- Omnipresent effect

## The Harvester Strategy (Evergreen at Scale)

### Purpose
An evergreen "field" of customers to harvest from. Most advertisers just pitch-pitch-pitch (like a farmer skipping planting/tending and going straight to harvest).

### The Full Sequence

**Step 1: Video 1 (Engagement + Video View)**
- Introduce personality/company with content
- Establish curiosity, authority, social proof in under 60 seconds
- Test 2-3 different Video 1's
- Use engagement AND video view campaigns (reaches different people)
- Set up warm audiences at 5-day, 10-day, every 5-day increment up to 365 days

**Step 2: Cycle Bin 1 (Days 1-5) -- Content Framing**
- Group of videos for warm audience
- Frame beliefs, shape viewpoints, increase buying probability
- Goal: authority bias, social proof, belief shaping over 3-5 days
- Target KPI: frequency of 3-5 in these 3-5 days
- Add as many videos as you want (AI targets each person with most relevant)
- Audience: 3-5 day warm audience (FB interactions, IG interactions, video viewers)

**Step 3: Direct Response + Retargeting (Days 5-30)**
- Now make your pitch
- At least 5-10 variations for each placement
- Build creative for every placement type
- Audience: warm audience of days 5-30

**Step 4: Cycle Bin 2 -- Belief Management During Pitching**
- Same audience being pitched also gets content
- Up to 20-25 belief-shaping videos
- Runs alongside direct response campaigns
- Frame them to view you optimally for highest conversion probability

**Step 5: Post-Opt-In (Messenger + Email + Trust Funnel)**
- Facebook Messenger opt-in via ManyChat
- Marketing automation for both email and messenger
- 2-day trust funnel: 10-20 minute value videos
- Videos can contain light CTA for main offer
- Use webinar funnel with on-demand feature (higher attendance)

**Step 6: Time-Based Retargeting**
- Use ConnectRetarget to retarget based on time spent on page:
  - < 30 seconds: "You didn't stay to watch" creative
  - < 2 min, 5 min, 10 min: contextual follow-up based on drop-off point
  - > 60 min, 90 min: drive directly to sales page

**Step 7: Post-Webinar Retargeting Sequence**
- Audiences: sales page visitors who didn't buy
- 5-7 day intervals between each retargeting ad
- 20+ different creatives with 20+ different hooks/angles
- Mirror in email and messenger sequences
- Run for up to 180 days (FB limit for targeting)
- Map out 20+ reasons someone would buy (like Lamborghini: up to 25 reasons, not just "go fast")

**Step 8: Post-Purchase (Days 1-5)**
- Target thank you page visitors for 3-5 days
- Treat them like customers -- no upsells yet
- Videos about product demos, customer successes, best practices
- Increases satisfaction and lowers attrition on recurring products

**Step 9: Cycle Bin 3 (Days 5-180) -- Customer Nurture**
- Thank you page visitors days 5-180 (exclude days 1-5)
- Keep brand omnipresent
- Reinforce their new reality
- Brand values, strong messaging, core knowledge
- Good time to upsell: 15-45 days after initial purchase

## Retargeting Campaign Budget

- Content warming campaigns (VFT/Harvester): $0.001-$0.02 per engagement
- Retargeting direct response: $5-10/day minimum
- Need audience of at least 100 for retargeting to be effective
- Low-budget daily campaigns that increase overall effectiveness

---

## Key Retargeting Rules

1. **Never over-pitch** -- balance direct response with content to avoid ad blindness
2. **20+ hooks and angles** in retargeting sequence -- people buy for different reasons
3. **Time-based targeting** reveals intent level (more time on page = higher intent)
4. **Post-purchase nurture first, upsell later** (3-5 days of treating like a customer)
5. **The Harvester is evergreen** -- set it up and keep harvesting
6. **Always be testing** -- new content, new angles, new sequences
