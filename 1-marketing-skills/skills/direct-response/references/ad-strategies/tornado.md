# The Tornado Ad Strategy

Source: Jeremy Haynes / Megalodon Marketing
Type: Content Distribution + Direct Response Hybrid Strategy
Best For: Any business with existing organic content (reels, TikToks, long-form) that wants to replicate the organic sales process at paid advertising scale. Tested with live webinars, evergreen webinars, call funnels, lead forms, ecom offers, high ticket offers, low ticket offers.

---

## The Problem It Solves

Sales people consistently compare paid leads to organic lead quality and struggle to close paid deals. With organic leads, the prospect has consumed an untold amount of content plus referral conversations, search engine research, YouTube content, etc. BEFORE talking to sales. With paid advertising, the sales conversation happens FIRST and content consumption typically follows.

**The Solution:** Instead of attacking the salesperson's skills, play into what they want. Think of yourself as working with the worst of the worst sales people -- make their role that of a cashier.

**The Tornado replicates the organic sales process.** Target the same ideal customers with direct response ads AND simultaneously target them with hundreds of pieces of content. Leads get hit with 5-12+ pieces of content per week while being targeted by direct response ads.

---

## The Goals of Content Distribution

- Establish familiarity bias
- Overcome potential objections
- Reduce the sales cycle timeline
- Increase ROAS
- Increase average order value and lifetime value
- Reduce churn
- Create and plant the right beliefs/views on company/product/service
- Increase overall reach to potential buyers for far less spend
- Create massive retargeting list of warm buyers
- Sales people giving feedback that leads are just as warm (if not warmer) compared to organic
- Generate large social proof on all content posted
- Generate followers

---

## The 3-Layer Process

### Layer 1: Cold Content Campaign
- 50-500+ pieces of content
- Target: cold customer audiences (same as normal direct response targeting)
- Exclude: warm audiences
- Intention: get cold audiences retargetable for $0.01 or less

### Layer 2: Warm Content Campaign
- 200-500+ pieces of content
- Target: warm audiences (365 FB interaction, 365 IG interaction, 365 TikTok interaction, 180 website visitors, email list)
- Goal: keep 5-12 pieces of content in front of audience per week for $0.05 or less per engagement/ThruPlay

### Layer 3: Direct Response Ads
- Lead magnets, webinars, call funnels, sales pages, etc.
- As many as you want running simultaneously

**The Key:** Content in front of potential buyers before, during, and after they interact with direct response. Achieve 5-12 frequency on 7-day window with warm audience.

## Content Selection

### Source
- Pull from Instagram Reels (ideally auto cross-posted to Facebook)
- Also select TikTok content
- Use ALL content available -- company, products, services, customer experiences, brand experiences
- Content that overcomes objections and removes buying friction
- Select every reel/TikTok that is not holiday-themed, going as far back as possible

### Why Reels/Short-Form
- Placements (stories, feeds, explore, reels, in-stream, banners) are most cost effective
- Getting the most attention right now
- Where users hang out in social media apps

## Building the Engagement Campaign (Cold)

### Campaign Level
- Objective: Engagement
- Ad set: Post Engagement
- Performance goal: Maximize engagement with a post

### Ad Set Level (Cold)
- Same targeting as cold direct response
- **EXCLUDE warm audiences:** Website Visitors 180, FB 365 All, IG 365 All

### Placements
- If reels cross-posted to FB: uncheck audience network, leave all IG and FB selected
- If not cross-posted: IG Reels, IG Feed, IG Stories, IG Explore only
- On TikTok: only TikTok native (no Pangle/Global App Bundle)

### Ad Level
- Select "Use Existing Post"
- 1 piece of content per ad
- **50 ads per ad set limit** on FB/IG

### Scaling Beyond 50 Pieces
- Hit 50 ads -> duplicate ad set -> select next 50 pieces
- Repeat until all content loaded

### Real Example: 383 Pieces of Content
- Client: $300K+/month spender, content machine
- 8 total cold ad sets (7 x 50 + 1 x 33)
- All targeting cold audiences, excluding warm

## Warm Audience Targeting (Two Paths)

### Path 1: Easy and Fast (1-5 minutes)
- Duplicate cold ad sets
- Remove cold targeting and warm exclusions
- Switch to warm custom audiences
- **Uncheck "Advantage custom audience"** -- only target warm
- Pros: fast. Cons: same person may see same content repeatedly

### Path 2: Guaranteed Unique Content (1-4 hours)
- Create 3-second video view audience for EACH video
- Each ad set excludes the 3-second view audience for that video
- 1 existing post per ad set matching the excluded audience
- Duplicate ad set for each piece of content, changing video + exclusion
- **Result:** Once someone watches 3 seconds, they never see it again -- only other content

**Why Path 2 not needed for cold:** Cold objective is bringing people into warm bucket. Warm objective is hammering them with dozens of pieces per month.

## Direct Response Setup

- Campaign optimized for leads or sales
- ABO
- 2 ad sets: cold + warm
- Cold: same targeting as content, exclude warm
- Warm: same warm targeting as content
- Split test to ensure warm generates cheaper cost per result

---

## Content KPIs

### Short Form (60-90 second reels/TikToks)
- Cost per 100% playthrough: ~$0.01-$0.10
- Higher total play-throughs vs long form

### Long Form (1-2 hour podcasts)
- Keep in SEPARATE ad sets from short form (otherwise short form dominates)
- Cost per 95-100% view on 2-hour content: ~$1.05
- Use newsfeed placement only (not reels)
- Both run simultaneously in different ad sets targeting same people
