# The Harvester Ad Strategy

Source: Jeremy Haynes / Megalodon Marketing
Type: Evergreen Ad Strategy for Scale
Best For: Heavy advertisers who can scale to millions in ad spend per month. Personal brands, companies, anyone with high quality social media content wanting to maximize reach on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, LinkedIn, YouTube.

---

## Core Concept

Most advertisers go straight for the close -- pitch, pitch, pitch, hoping to convert 2% and barely profit. They are farmers who skip planting, tending, watering, and go straight for the harvest.

You can run out of customers if you only target those ready to buy NOW. The Harvester creates an evergreen "field" of customers to harvest from by making people aware, building trust, and finding commonality with your brand so you convert them in shorter periods when ready to transact.

---

## Why It Works

- Social interaction is retargetable on Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn
- Modern-day email list building inside social channels for 100X-1000X cheaper
- Open rates near 100% because you are in their newsfeed (vs email open rates)
- 53% of all internet attention goes to social media; 83% of content consumption is video
- Average cost per video view/ThruPlay/engagement: $0.001-$0.02
- Competition spending average $1.00 driving people to their website
- Your dollar goes 100X-1000X further than competitors using direct response only

## The 7-Phase Process

### Phase 1: Video 1 (Cold Audience Introduction)

Start with a specific piece of content introducing the personality brand or company. Backed by engagement campaign + video view campaign targeted at ideal customers.

**Video 1 Purpose:** Establish curiosity, authority, and social proof in under 60 seconds.

**Testing:** Test 2-3 different Video 1s. Use both engagement campaigns AND video view campaigns optimized for ThruPlay (charges at 15 seconds). The algorithm reaches two different types of people -- watchers vs engagers.

**Audience Setup:** Create warm audiences with 5-day increments up to 365 days:
- Website traffic
- Social interaction on Facebook and Instagram
- Video view audiences
- Custom audiences (email lists)

Tool for bulk audience creation: audaccelerator.crmsecureorders.com

### Phase 2: Content Cycle Bin 1 (Warming, Days 0-5)

After Video 1, drive warm audience to Content Cycle Bin 1.

**Cycle Bin:** A group of videos put in front of specific audiences to frame them, shape beliefs/viewpoints, and increase probability they respond to offers.

**Purpose:** Establish more authority bias, social proof. Shape beliefs over 3-5 days.
**Goal KPI:** Frequency of 3-5 in these 3-5 days.
**Content Volume:** Put as many videos as you want. AI targets warm audience with videos they are most likely to engage with.
**Audience:** Warm audience that is 3-5 days big. Facebook interactions, Instagram interactions, video view audiences.

### Phase 3: Direct Response + Content Cycle Bin 2 (Days 5-30)

Set up direct response ads driving to lead magnet/webinar.

**Retargeting ads:** 5-10 variations for each placement. Build creative for: right side column, Facebook newsfeed, Instagram newsfeed, story placements (FB/IG/Messenger), Messenger, in-stream, marketplace, audience network.

**Audience for direct response:** Warm audience of days 5-30.

**Content Cycle Bin 2:** Target same audience seeing direct response. Up to 20-25 different videos. Content shapes beliefs and manages perceptions for highest conversion probability.

### Phase 4: Trust Funnel (Post Opt-In)

Once someone opts in:
- Facebook Messenger opt-in via Manychat
- Marketing automation for email AND Messenger
- 2-day trust funnel: videos 10-20 minutes long with additional value
- Trust funnel videos can contain light CTAs at the end
- If using CTA to schedule call, pepper throughout video a few times

### Phase 5: Webinar / Conversion Event

Leverage webinar with "just in time" or "on demand" feature (attendance rate increases with on demand).

**Time-based retargeting (Connectio.io):**
- On page < 30 seconds: retarget calling them out for not watching
- On page < 2 min, 5 min, 10 min: contextual follow-up based on drop-off
- On page > 60 min, 90 min: drive directly to sales page

### Phase 6: Retargeting Sequence (Post-Sales Page, up to 180 days)

Target audiences that visited sales page and did not buy:
- **5-7 day intervals** between audiences targeted with each retargeting ad
- **20+ different creatives** using 20+ different hooks and angles
- Ad sets spaced 5-7 days apart
- Same approach in email and Messenger follow-up
- Campaign runs up to **180 days** (FB targeting limit)

**Key insight:** People buy for different reasons. Protein powder = 1-2 reasons. Lamborghini = 25+ reasons. Map out all 20+ reasons someone buys your product and create ads for each buying motive.

### Phase 7: Content Cycle Bin 3 (Post-Purchase)

Treat customers like customers for 3-5 days:
- Product demos, customer successes, best practices
- Audience: thank you page traffic for 3-5 day duration
- Do NOT upsell yet -- just nurture

**After nurture phase (days 5-180):**
- Keep brand omnipresent
- Audience: thank you page visitors days 5-180
- Content re-enforces new reality, primes for next product
- Upsell timing: 15-45 days from initial purchase depending on product

## Key Principles

1. The Harvester is evergreen once set up
2. Always be testing
3. Respect the sales process -- do not just pitch 98% of your audience
4. Dollar goes 100X-1000X further than competitors doing direct response only
5. Build audiences in 5-day increments up to 365 days
6. 20+ hooks and angles for retargeting (different buying motives)
7. Trust funnel videos: 10-20 minutes of pure value
8. Treat customers like customers before upselling

## Audience Setup (Critical Detail)

### 5-Day Increment Audiences

Set up audiences with different date ranges in 5-day increments up to 365 days. This is tedious but worth it.

**Why 5-day increments:**
- Precise targeting at each stage of buyer journey
- Separates brand new warm (0-5 days) from people warmed up weeks ago
- Enables different content and messaging at each window
- Prevents over-pitching to brand new warm audiences
- Ensures direct response only hits people with enough warming time

**Audience types at each increment:**
- Website traffic (5, 10, 15, 20... up to 180 days -- platform limit)
- Facebook page interactions (up to 365 days)
- Instagram profile interactions (up to 365 days)
- Video view audiences by percentage (25%, 50%, 75%, 95%)
- Custom audiences from email lists

### Content Selection by Bin

**Bin 1 (Cold to Warm):** Authority, social proof, value content. Zero pitch. 3-5 frequency in 3-5 days.

**Bin 2 (During Direct Response):** 20-25 videos. Supports the pitch without pitching. Handles objections. Reinforces beliefs. Manages perceptions about price, quality, legitimacy.

**Bin 3 (Post-Purchase):** Product walkthroughs, customer success stories, best practices, community, brand values.

## Retargeting Deep Dive (Phase 6)

### The Multi-Reason Framework

Map ALL reasons someone would buy. Not just the obvious.

**Example: High-Ticket Coaching Program (20 Reasons)**
1. Want more money
2. Prove doubters wrong
3. Escape current job
4. Freedom and flexibility
5. Provide better for family
6. Community of like-minded people
7. Accountability
8. Step-by-step system
9. Credibility from known brand
10. Fear of being left behind
11. Guilt about not reaching potential
12. Escape from monotony
13. Status of exclusive program
14. Hope for better future
15. Need structure and deadlines
16. Impress spouse/partner
17. Competitive drive
18. Transformation story for social
19. External validation
20. Fear of regretting not trying

Each becomes a different retargeting creative. 5-7 day intervals between each.

### Placement Strategy

Build creative for ALL placements: right side column, FB newsfeed, IG newsfeed, FB Stories, IG Stories, Messenger Stories, Messenger inbox, in-stream video, Marketplace, audience network.

Best practice: 5-10 variations per placement.

## Trust Funnel Video Framework (Phase 4)

**Video 1 (Day 1):** Empathy for situation. Origin story. Massive tactical value. Light CTA.

**Video 2 (Day 2):** Detailed client success story. Specific steps. Address #1 objection. Light CTA.

**Video 3 (Day 3, optional):** Behind-the-scenes. Show the work. Handle secondary objections. Stronger CTA.

Send via email AND Messenger.
