# Hiring & Team Onboarding SOP

Source: MIM - Jeremy Haynes

**Department:** Hiring Manager
**Tools:** Culture Index survey, Kajabi/training platform, DocuSign

---

## Step 1: Generate Qualified Applicants

### a. In Network (Preferred)
- Facebook groups where people can vouch for applicants
- More accountability for the hire
- Referrals from talent trainers/sourcers

### b. Facebook Post + Engagement Campaign
- Post about the open position
- Run engagement ad targeting ideal applicants
- Ask for 60-second video + resume
- Jeremy's example post generated: 5,728 reach, 1,069 engagements, 153 reactions

### c. ZipRecruiter
- Posts across all job boards

### d. Recruiter Agency (Premium)
- For highly qualified talent you're willing to pay premium for
- Robert Half or similar headhunting firms

### e. "Silicon Valley" Package (Last Resort)
- Sign-on bonus + benefits + lifestyle perks
- Only for headhunting when other methods fail

---

## Step 2: Two Interview Rounds

### First Interview
- Background, skills assessment, NDA/non-compete awareness
- Vet for results orientation
- Assess cultural fit

### Second Interview (Critical Thinking Test)
- Scenario-based questions: "How would you handle [challenging situation]?"
- Political assessment: "If your manager won't allow something, would you go around them?"
- Character questions: "Would you eat someone else's lunch from the fridge?"
- Punctuality: "What time do you show up for a 2 PM zoom?"
- **What you're looking for:** thinking ability. Skills are trainable. Thinking is not.

---

## Step 3: Culture Index Survey
- Measures personality, motivational needs, communication preferences
- Ensures good fit for both parties
- Guides management approach for the hire
- Traits: risk tolerance, communication style, task preference, compliance, emotional influence, imaginative thinking

---

## Step 4: Onboarding (90-Day Probation)
- Reduced pay during probationary period
- Testing period for both parties
- Have them sign: NDA, non-compete, 1099 contractor form, contractor agreement
- All via DocuSign -- refuse to sign = refuse to hire

---

## Steps 5-14: Training & Integration

5. **Initial assignments** -- start with lowest-hanging revenue tasks
6. **Company email and tools** -- project management, ad platforms, etc.
7. **Team communication** -- add to Slack/Telegram
8. **Training videos in Kajabi** -- tracked, with assignments
9. **SOPs** -- written procedures for every responsibility
10. **Weekly team meeting** -- life/mindset skills training as a unit
11. **Questions culture** -- questions don't make them look dumb. Schedule open office hours
12. **Open culture** -- no harsh criticism or attacks
13. **Track by statistics** -- revenue-driven metrics to justify payroll
14. **End of 90 days** -- renegotiate or roll into 6-month/1-year agreement

---

## Key Principles
- Every-other-day 1-hour training sessions during onboarding
- Measure productivity against revenue, not hours worked
- Create SOPs on the fly for anything they ask about
- Start with repetitive tasks they can execute while learning
