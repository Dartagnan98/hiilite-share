# Client Reporting SOP

Source: Jeremy Haynes - Digital Marketing Manuscript 2.0

**Department:** Account Manager
**Time:** 15 minutes/week
**Goal:** Weekly report showing how you drove revenue for the client

---

## Tools Needed
- Ad channel data
- Third-party reporting (WickedReports, Google Analytics, Hyros, CRM)
- Email client (Gmail)

---

## Weekly Report Process

### Step 1: Create Email
- To: client + assigned salesperson + relevant team members
- Subject: "Weekly Report"

### Step 2: Start with Overall ROI
- Lead with spend vs revenue generated
- Use offline conversion reports if needed (not just ad manager data)
- Cross-check accuracy:
  - CRM tags match ad manager reported conversions
  - Form tools (JotForm, Typeform) match reported applications
  - Calendar tools (Calendly, ScheduleOnce) match reported calls
- If discrepancy found: do manual math, export data, hand to VA for visualizations

### Step 3: Intro with Visual
Example: "The month of April was the best month yet! 62 deals closed, producing over $91,312 in revenue!"
- Pair with performance screenshot/chart

### Step 4: Changes and Campaigns
- Highlight changes made this week
- New campaigns launched
- A/B test results
- Client-specific KPIs (varies per client)

### Step 5: Highlight Contributors
- Shout out salespeople who closed deals
- Include conversion stats: close rate, lead cost, deal count
- Example: "Huge shoutout to Riley and Kyle -- 203 leads at $49/lead, 62 closed at 30% close ratio!"

### Step 6: Close with Overall ROI
- Monthly spend-to-revenue summary
- Include quarterly data when applicable
- Example: "$11,836 spent, $91,312 revenue = 671.4% ROI, net $79,476"
- Quarterly: "$20,077 spent, $172,034 revenue = 756% ROI, net $151,957"

### Step 7: Sign Off
- Professional signature

### Step 8: Bad Week Handling
- Close with "here's what revenue-driven actions we're taking this week"
- Give client clear picture of the recovery plan
- List specific actions: new campaigns launching, retargeting changes, split tests, funnel optimization

---

## Reporting Best Practices

1. **Use their preferred method** -- integrate into their existing reporting system
2. **Use 3rd party reporting** -- ad managers are universally inaccurate. Use Hyros, WickedReports, CRM, or funnel analytics
3. **Separate funnels per channel** -- duplicate funnels for FB/IG, organic, Google, TikTok. Keeps analytics clean
4. **Multiple analytics sources** -- always have backup data for discrepancies
5. **Short-term + long-term view** -- one bad week doesn't mean a bad month. Show both perspectives
6. **If good:** show how you'll keep growing
7. **If bad:** show how you'll improve

### Cautionary Tale: CRM Tag Disaster
- Client had 714% ROI over 7 months
- Lost the deal because Zapier tags were wrong -- buyers tagged as organic instead of FB/IG
- Client only believed CRM tag data, not Hyros or offline conversion data
- All the data in the world didn't matter because client trusted the wrong reporting source
- **Lesson:** Be in the same world as your client on reporting from day one
