# Show Rate & Connection Rate Mastery

Source: MIM - Sales Show Rate Mastery (Jeremy Haynes)

---

## Core Philosophy

The sales process starts BEFORE the call. Most people focus on closing but ignore the biggest levers: getting people to show up and making a real connection before pitching.

## Show Rate Benchmarks

| Level | Rate |
|-------|------|
| Bad | Below 50% |
| Average | 50-65% |
| Good | 65-80% |
| Elite | 80-95% |

**Formula:** Show Rate = (People who showed / People who booked) x 100

- Doubling show rate doubles revenue without extra ad spend
- 50% show rate = throwing away half your ad spend

## Pre-Call Show Rate Strategies

### 1. Speed to Lead
- Contact within 5 minutes of booking
- After 5 minutes, odds drop by 10x
- After 30 minutes, odds of qualifying drop by 21x
- First person to contact wins 78% of the time

### 2. Confirmation Sequence

**Immediately after booking:**
- Confirmation email with call details
- Text message confirmation
- Thank you page with video

**Thank You Page Video Script:**
1. Congratulate them on taking action
2. Explain exactly what to expect on the call
3. Give a small homework assignment
4. Create excitement about the transformation
5. Remind them of the pain they're trying to solve

**24 hours before:** Reminder email + text + meeting link
**1 hour before:** "Looking forward to speaking in 60 minutes"
**15 minutes before:** "We're about to get started!"

### 3. Indoctrination Content
Between booking and call, send:
- Case study videos
- Testimonial compilations
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Educational/authority content
- "What to expect on your call" video

### 4. Pre-Call Survey / Application
- Make them invest time before the call
- Ask qualifying questions (goals, situation, budget)
- More investment pre-call = higher show rate
- Also pre-qualifies to avoid wasted time

### 5. Calendar Hygiene
- Keep booking windows tight (24-72 hours ideal)
- Don't let people book 2+ weeks out
- Monday/Tuesday calls have higher show rates
- Morning calls outperform afternoon

## Day-of Tactics

- Call if 2 minutes late
- If no answer, text immediately
- If still nothing, email
- **Triple-tap within 5 minutes:** call + text + email
- Offer to reschedule immediately
- Never give up after one no-show -- follow up 3-5 times

### Reschedule Protocol
- **Within 5 min:** Call + text + email
- **Same day:** Follow up 2-4 hours later
- **Next day:** "No worries, life happens" angle
- **Day 3:** "I held a spot for you -- want to reschedule?"
- **After 3 days:** Move to nurture sequence, re-engage in 7-14 days

## Connection Rate Framework

### Step 1: Set the Frame (2-3 min)
- Thank them for showing up
- Acknowledge their time is valuable
- Tell them exactly how the call will go
- Ask permission to ask questions
- Set expectation: "By the end, one of three things happens..."
  1. Great fit, we move forward
  2. Not a fit, totally fine
  3. Need more info to decide

### Step 2: Deep Discovery (15-20 min)
This is where 90% of the sale happens. NOT pitching -- listening.

**Key Questions:**
- "What made you book this call today?"
- "What's going on in your business/life right now?"
- "How long has this been a problem?"
- "What have you tried before? Why didn't those work?"
- "What would it mean if this was solved?"
- "What happens if nothing changes?"
- "Where do you want to be in 6-12 months?"

**Rules:**
- Listen 80% of the time (80/20 rule)
- Repeat back what they say
- Don't rush to solution mode
- Dig deeper with "tell me more about that"
- Find the emotional driver, not just logical

### Step 3: Transition to Presentation
- Summarize their situation
- Confirm: "Did I capture that accurately?"
- Bridge: "Based on everything you've shared, I think I can help..."

### Step 4: Present the Solution (10-15 min)
- Frame around THEIR specific problems
- Use their words back to them
- Connect every feature to their specific desired outcome
- Include relevant social proof
- Don't feature-dump

### Step 5: Handle Objections (see objection-handling.md)

### Step 6: Close
- Be direct: "Based on our conversation, here's what I recommend..."
- State price confidently
- Ask: "Want to get started?"
- SHUT UP after asking (let them respond)
- If yes, take payment immediately
- If no, find out why and address

## Advanced Strategies

### VSLs Before Calls
- 10-20 minute VSL between booking and call
- Educates, builds authority, pre-handles objections
- Track who watches full VSL vs who doesn't
- Full VSL watchers close at 2-3x higher rates

### Social Proof Stacking
- 1 relevant case study per day between booking and call
- Match case study to their industry/situation
- Video testimonials outperform written by 5x

### The "Burning Platform" Technique
- Quantify cost of inaction during discovery
- "You said you're losing $X/month. Over 12 months that's $Y. Our program is $Z. You're paying more to NOT solve this."

### Post-Call Follow Up (Non-Closes)
- Personalized video within 1 hour
- Reference specific things they said
- Include direct purchase link
- Follow up at 24h, 48h, and 7 days

## 10 Rules

1. The sale starts at the ad, not on the call
2. Speed to lead is the single highest-leverage improvement
3. Nobody cares about features until they feel heard
4. The best closers are the best listeners
5. Follow up is where most revenue hides
6. Confidence comes from belief in your product, not scripts
7. Fix show rate before hiring more setters (if below 70%)
8. Every no-show = real dollars wasted on ads
9. Pre-call indoctrination does 50% of the selling
10. Track everything or you're flying blind
