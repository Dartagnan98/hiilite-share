## HEADLINE WRITING RULES

1. **Self-Interest First**: Every headline answers "what's in it for me?"
2. **Specificity Wins**: "$10,000/month" beats "more money." Numbers, timeframes, details always outperform
3. **Target The Right Audience**: Call out who you're talking to. If talking to dentists, say "dentists"
4. **Combine Elements**: Best headlines combine 2-3 categories
   - Example: Question + Number + Identity = "Are You Making These 5 Mistakes That Cost Real Estate Agents Thousands?"
5. **Test Everything**: Headlines are the single most impactful element. Test 5-10 variations minimum
6. **First Line = First Impression**: If the headline doesn't stop the scroll, nothing else matters
7. **Avoid Clickbait Without Substance**: Curiosity alone = clickbait that damages trust. Combine with self-interest or news.
8. **Match Awareness Level**: Unaware = education-first. Aware = specific claims and offers
