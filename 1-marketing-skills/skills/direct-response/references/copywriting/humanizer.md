# Humanizer: Remove AI Writing Patterns
**Source: Merged from humanizer skill (Wikipedia's "Signs of AI writing")**

---

## Process
1. Identify AI patterns in text
2. Rewrite problematic sections
3. Preserve meaning, maintain voice
4. Add personality -- sterile voiceless writing is just as obvious as slop
5. Final anti-AI pass: "What makes this obviously AI?" then fix remaining tells

## Adding Soul
- Have opinions, don't just report facts
- Vary rhythm: short punchy sentences mixed with longer ones
- Acknowledge complexity and mixed feelings
- Use "I" when it fits
- Let some mess in -- perfect structure feels algorithmic
- Be specific about feelings, not generic

## Content Patterns to Fix

### Significance Inflation
Kill: "stands/serves as", "testament", "pivotal", "crucial", "underscores", "evolving landscape", "indelible mark"
Fix: State what it IS, not what it REPRESENTS.

### Superficial -ing Phrases
Kill: "highlighting...", "ensuring...", "reflecting...", "contributing to...", "showcasing..."
Fix: Remove them. They add fake depth.

### Promotional Language
Kill: "boasts", "vibrant", "profound", "groundbreaking", "renowned", "breathtaking", "nestled"
Fix: Use neutral, specific language.

### Vague Attributions
Kill: "Industry reports", "Experts argue", "Some critics"
Fix: Name specific sources or remove.

## Language Patterns to Fix

### AI Vocabulary (overused post-2023)
Additionally, align with, crucial, delve, emphasizing, enduring, enhance, fostering, garner, highlight, interplay, intricate, landscape (abstract), pivotal, showcase, tapestry (abstract), testament, underscore, valuable, vibrant

### Copula Avoidance
Kill: "serves as", "stands as", "marks", "features"
Fix: Just use "is" or "has"

### Negative Parallelisms
Kill: "Not only...but...", "It's not just about..., it's..."

### Rule of Three
Kill forced groups of three. Two is fine. Four is fine.

### Synonym Cycling
Kill: protagonist/main character/central figure/hero in same paragraph

## Style Patterns to Fix
- Em dash overuse -- use commas or periods instead
- Excessive boldface -- remove mechanical emphasis
- Inline-header lists (**Speed:** The speed...) -- convert to prose
- Title Case In Every Heading -- use sentence case
- Emojis in headers/bullets -- remove all

## Communication Artifacts to Remove
- "I hope this helps!", "Certainly!", "Great question!"
- "Would you like me to expand on..."
- "As of [date]", "based on available information"
- Sycophantic tone ("You're absolutely right!")

## Filler to Cut
- "In order to" -> "To"
- "Due to the fact that" -> "Because"
- "At this point in time" -> "Now"
- "It is important to note that" -> (delete, just state it)

## Generic Conclusions to Kill
- "The future looks bright"
- "Exciting times lie ahead"
- "This represents a major step"
Fix: End with a specific fact or next action.
