## Definition
A **money model** is a series of offers.
A **good money model** is a series of offers structured to:
1. Minimize CAC
2. Maximize gross profit
3. Collected in the first 30 days

## The Rental Car Story (Money Model in Action)
Started at $19/day reservation. Left paying $400 for 3 days.

Sequence of 7 offers:
1. **Car upgrade** -- "We don't have your reserved car. Is a Tundra okay?" ($19/day -> $69/day)
2. **Late checkout** -- half-day fee to avoid late return penalty (fee they invented)
3. **Insurance upgrade** -- "Do you want to upgrade your insurance?" (implied you already have minimum)
4. **Minimum insurance** -- "So just the minimum then?" (implied you must get it because it's "minimum")
5. **Prepaid gas** -- $3.75/gal vs. market $3.50 (buy the FULL tank upfront; whatever you don't use = pure profit)
6. **Damages** -- implicit upsell (scratch it, you pay)
7. **Late return fee** -- yet another invented penalty

**Key lessons:**
- "Minimum package" naming = masterful. 90% of people take it because it sounds required
- Rental car companies invented the fees, then sell avoidance of those fees
- Making 5+ offers in one conversation is normal. 19/20 customers don't mind.

## Gym Launch Full Money Model Flow
1. **Front end**: Free book, lead magnet, podcast

## Downsell Flows

### Main Service Downsell (for people who said no)
Sell to goal -> smaller package -> free trial with penalty -> free trial no commitment -> free orientation
- Even "no" customers got sold $300 in supplements at nutrition orientation

### Supplement Downsell Flow
Full bundle -> 4-pack with extras -> 4-pack only -> continuity -> no continuity -> extras only

### Continuity Downsell Flow
Sell to goal -> smaller package -> month-to-month

### Prepayment Downsell Flow
Prepay whole package -> prepay with discount -> prepay next month -> just get the commitment

## How to Build Your Own Money Model (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1: Start with an Attraction Offer
- Get leads to engage with you

### Step 2: Pick an Upsell Offer
- Menu upsell, anchor upsell, rollover upsell, or classic

### Step 3: Pick a Downsell Offer
- For people who say no: payment plan -> feature downsell -> free trial

### Step 4: Pick a Continuity Offer
- Get cash back in 30 days, more customers, more money

### Step 5: Perfect One at a Time
- Adding all four at once will break your business

### Rules
1. Perfect ONE at a time. Adding all four at once will break your business.
2. Start with a lower price, get yeses, raise prices in stages.
3. Simple scales, fancy fails. Fewest offers possible.
4. Affiliate products can fill gaps without operational cost.
5. Any attraction offer becomes continuity by adding a rollover clause.
6. A business model is NOT patentable. It's a speed game -- innovate, extract, repeat.

### Fitness Model
- Front end: Win Your Money Back offer
- Upsell: Buy X Get Y Free (prepaying time)

- Attraction: Free Trial

- Back end: Consulting upsell
