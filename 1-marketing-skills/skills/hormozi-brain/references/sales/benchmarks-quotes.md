## Professional Development Framework (Three Levels)

1. **Skills** -- Enter marketplace with nothing. Must acquire: copywriting, creative, landing pages, traffic, lead nurture, appointment setting, sales scripting, tonality, objection handling, payment processing, etc.

2. **Character Traits** -- Once you have tools, you need patience, endurance, discipline to consistently execute. Separates moderately successful from very successful.

3. **Beliefs** -- Beliefs about the world, market, what's possible. Tolerance of what's acceptable in speed and excellence. Top-down from the leader.

"It's cyclical. Every level encompasses all three. You spin through them over and over. You must maintain the skills, beliefs, and traits of the base below before reaching the next level."

## ADDITIONAL BENCHMARKS (Deep Pass -- Batches 3-4)

- Frontline position churn: ~20% per quarter
- Average small business owner income: ~$78K/year
- Hormozi's salespeople make $150-200K/year (2-2.5x average business owner)

### Close Rate to Pricing Guide
- 60-80% close rate = 3-5x price increase sitting there
- 50-60% = 2-3x available
- 30-50% = appropriately priced
- Below 30% = selling skill issue

### Conversion Benchmarks by Format

- Webinar: 10% of live attendees
- Strategy call: 25%+ of qualified calls
- Chiropractor dinner: 25% buy consult, 1/3 of those buy $5-10K = ~10% of room buys big thing

### Salesperson Income Maximization Playbook
1. Live on salary only, invest all variable comp
2. Pay off high-interest debt first (credit cards)
3. Dollar cost average into S&P 500 monthly
4. Track net worth daily
5. Tie yourself to an acquisition channel
6. Find a channel the company isn't using, master it, build a team under you
7. Understand volatility vs risk

### Volatility vs Risk
- Insurance company: loses money 1 out of 9 years (catastrophe), makes a lot the other 8
- "My insurance business is not risky. It is volatile. Those are not the same things."
- Sales is highly volatile but LOW risk -- salespeople generate revenue, most secure employees
- "If you want to decrease the volatility in your life, decrease your expenses to your salary."
