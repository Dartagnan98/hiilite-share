# Diagnostics
## Source: Alex Hormozi (383 video transcripts)

## Multiple Metrics for Success + Constraints

### Beyond Profit
- First metric: profit and net worth increase
- As marginal utility of money decreases, add constraints
- "Can I build a billion dollar portfolio working 2-3 days a week?"
- "Can I do that while being in competitive physical shape?"
- Constraints make goals more interesting without breaking the machine

### Two Phases
1. First learn to sacrifice everything to achieve the goal
2. Then add constraints to make the game harder and improve life in other areas
- "You have to achieve mastery over the thing before you can truly give it away"

## Being Stuck: Beliefs, Traits, Skills Framework

### Why Entrepreneurs Get Stuck
- Three possible deficiencies:
  1. **Belief**: Incorrect belief about what's possible (e.g., "no one in my space does over $2M/month")
  2. **Trait**: Character trait deficiency (patience, persistence, genuine belief in product)
  3. **Skill**: Skill deficiency (don't know how to do something OR don't know how to find someone who can)

### Market Cap Test
- Add up all competitors' revenue -- that's what you could do if you were better than all of them
- If you haven't sold 30%+ of your marketplace, you haven't capped your market
- "You just have capped your skill or your beliefs or your traits"

### Product Belief Check
- Many people subconsciously stop growing because they don't genuinely believe their product is good enough
- They cap out at meeting their own individual needs
- Beyond personal needs, growth requires belief that the product helps others

## Speed / Execution Frameworks

### Being Fast Makes You Win
- Hard decisions are hard because you've already made all the easy ones
- "The only thing guaranteed is that if you never take that step back, you will never get the outsized return"
- Spell out both sides of the trade explicitly

### The Trade-Off Framework
- For any hard decision, explicitly spell out:
  - Option A: What you get + what you give up
  - Option B: What you get + what you give up
- Most people don't confront the trade -- they just stay stuck
- "The only thing guaranteed is that if you want everything from life, you will get nothing"
