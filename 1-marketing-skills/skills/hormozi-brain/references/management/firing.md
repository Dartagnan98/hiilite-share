# Firing Framework
## Source: Alex Hormozi (383 video transcripts)

## Hormozi's Firing Philosophy

- "We fire people like crazy, but people don't quit"
- "Low performers don't last"
- High performance culture = winning team that self-selects
- If someone new doesn't assimilate to the culture, the team rejects them
- "Gym Launch is a dangerous place to work -- if you are a winner, you will love it here"

## The Partner Who Fired Late People

- Locked the door at meeting time, fired everyone who arrived late
- "He reset the bar of this is how we perform"
- Set the tone for the entire culture

## 3-Week Rule (for trained frontline employees)

- "If you have trained someone properly and they are still below expectations three weeks in, cut them"
- Keeps culture high, shows team you respect the standard
- "No one feels like anyone is freeloading"
- Entry-level advertising jobs aren't complex -- they require traits (grit, endurance)
- Meta-skill of grit takes longer to train than the skill itself
- If below expectations after 3 weeks of proper training: cut them

## The Vince Story

- Hormozi worked at Smoothie King, trained by owner Dave to do everything right
- Manager "Vince" told him "we don't do any of that stuff"
- "Do you have a Vince? Someone secretly destroying your business?"
- Managers who lower the bar behind the owner's back are the biggest threat

## When to Fire Partners

- See partnerships reference for full framework
- If business isn't making money: both walk away
- Have the conversation directly: "You said you'd do sales, you're not doing sales. What do you think is fair?"
