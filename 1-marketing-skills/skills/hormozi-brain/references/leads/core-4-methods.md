# The Core Four Framework
## Source: Alex Hormozi ($100M Leads + 383 video transcripts)

## Core Definitions

### What Is a Lead
- **Lead**: A person you can contact. That is all. Anyone whose contact info you have (email, phone, DM, address) is a lead.
- **Engaged Lead**: A person you can contact WHO HAS SHOWN INTEREST in the stuff you sell. This is the true output of advertising.
- Someone follows you on social media and you can DM them = lead. They reply to your message or opt in = engaged lead.

### What Is Advertising
- **Advertising**: The process of making known. Ideally to people who don't know you yet.
- If you're not getting enough leads, you're not advertising enough. Simple.
- When all else is equal: double your leads = double your business. 10x your leads = 10x your business.

### Two Types of Audiences
1. **Warm Audience**: People who gave you permission to contact them. Friends, family, followers, past/current customers, contacts. They need to know YOU (unidirectional -- they know you, you don't necessarily know them).
2. **Cold Audience**: People who did NOT give you permission to contact them. Strangers. Other people's audiences/lists/eyeballs that belong to platforms.

### Two Types of Communication
1. **One-to-One** (private): DMs, calls, emails, texts
2. **One-to-Many** (public): Posts, ads, content, broadcasts

The 2x2 matrix of audience type x communication type creates the ONLY four ways one person can let other people know about anything:

These are 100% in your control. If you're not getting enough leads, it's because you're not doing enough of the Core Four.

## Method Overview

### 1. Warm Outreach (One-to-One, Warm)
- Reach out to people who already know you
- Use ACA framework (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask)
- Ask if they know anyone, not selling them directly
- Start free, raise prices as referrals come in
- Average 9 follow-up attempts needed

### 2. Post Content (One-to-Many, Warm)
- Content unit: Hook -> Retain -> Reward
- Give/Ask ratio: at minimum 4:1, ideally way beyond
- "Give in public, ask in private"
- Content is a massive multiplier on ALL other advertising methods
- 78% of Gym Launch customers consumed 2+ long-form pieces before buying

### 3. Cold Outreach (One-to-One, Cold)
- Reach out to strangers via calls, emails, DMs
- Three problems to solve: How to contact, What to say, Getting enough chances
- 100 cold calls/day -> ~4 engaged leads
- Competition can't see what you're doing (everything private)
- Incredibly reliable -- numbers work like clockwork

### 4. Paid Ads (One-to-Many, Cold)
- Three phases: Track Money -> Lose Money -> Print Money
- Benchmark: 3:1 LTGP-to-CAC ratio minimum
- If ads are working, there is no budget -- you spend as much as possible
- Constraint is never ad budget -- it's delivery capacity
- Out of 300 ads, maybe 5 will be ROI-positive, 1 will be a home run
