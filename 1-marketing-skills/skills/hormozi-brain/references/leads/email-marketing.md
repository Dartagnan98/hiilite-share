# Email Marketing
## Source: Alex Hormozi ($100M Leads + 383 video transcripts)

## Why Email
- Average ROI: $35-$45 return per $1 spent
- You already paid to acquire the contact. Email is just follow-up.
- 61% check email multiple times/day, 20% check multiple times/hour
- Uniform across ALL age groups (15-24, 25-44, 45-64, 65+)
- You control delivery (unlike algorithm-dependent social posts)

---

## Hormozi's Email Structure (Mosy Money Minute)
1. **Subject line**: Brand name + topic (building brand recognition)
2. **Opening quote/tweet**: Immediate reward for opening. Value in one glance.
3. **Meat**: Under 200 words (~1 minute read). ONE concept. One clear tactic.
4. **CTA**: Contextual to the content topic (beginners -> school.com, advanced -> acquisition.com/workshops)
5. **PS statement**: Meme, joke, or secondary CTA. "Not having a PS is PS stupid."

---

## Key Email Tactics

1. **Make unsubscribing easy**: Don't keep people captive. Dormant subscribers hurt deliverability worse than unsubscribes. First email will have higher unsubscribe (shaking the tree). Hormozi's: 1% first email -> 0.042% ongoing.

2. **Segment your list**: HubSpot research shows segmented lists get way higher returns. Send right content to right people (beginner vs advanced).

3. **Mobile-optimized design**: 58-60% of traffic is mobile. Check both desktop and mobile display.

4. **Consistent layout/template**: Figure out what works, repeat. People expect McDonald's consistency.

5. **Plain text, minimal links**: Emails should look like normal emails you'd exchange. No Vegas Strip design. Fewer images = better deliverability. Less money language = stays out of promo tab.

6. **Optimize preview text**: 24%+ of people check preview text before opening. Don't let it default to "Hey John..."

7. **Clear, contextual CTA**: Bridge the CTA to the content topic. Don't copy-paste the same generic CTA.

8. **AB test subject lines**: Most platforms can test a small segment, then send winner to full list.

9. **Get replies**: When someone replies, it signals the email server that you're legitimate. Ask a question that requires "yes" reply to unlock bonus content.

10. **Speed to contact**: Harvard Business Review: calling leads within 60 seconds of opt-in = 391% increase in sales (4x).

## Email Cadence
- Perfect cadence: ~3x/week (but not a rule)
- Start with once/month if you haven't been emailing
- Block out 12-24 emails at once (half a day of work = year of emails)
- More often you email = more you convert, as long as quality stays high
- Never commit to a cadence that forces low-quality output

## Email Benchmarks
- Hormozi's open rate: ~35.7% (last 90 days)
- Hormozi's CTR: 8.5% (very strong)
- B2B: 16.99% of marketing emails don't even reach inbox
- Best days: B2C = Monday/Tuesday, B2B = Wednesday
- Best times: 10am-noon and 1pm-3pm
