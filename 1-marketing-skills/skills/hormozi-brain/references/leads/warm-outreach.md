# Warm Outreach
## Source: Alex Hormozi ($100M Leads + 383 video transcripts)

## The ACA Framework (Acknowledge, Compliment, Ask)

### Step-by-Step Process

1. **Build your list from 3 sources**:
   - All email contacts (anyone you've ever emailed)
   - All social media followers you can DM (every platform)
   - All phone contacts (export them)
   - You will have WAY more leads than you think

2. **Pick the platform** where you have the most contacts. Start there.

3. **Personalize your message**: Take 30 seconds, look at their profile, reference something real (new baby, job change, recent post). Be a human.

4. **Reach out to 100 people per day**. It took you 20 years to send the first DM; it'll take 2 minutes to send the second.

5. **If they reply, use ACA**:
   - **Acknowledge**: "Oh so you have two kids"
   - **Compliment**: "You must be a super mom working full-time with two kids"
   - **Ask** (tie to compliment): "How do you find time to eat right and work out?"
   - They'll tell you stuff. Then: "By the way, do you know anyone looking for [dream outcome]? I'm opening up a few slots."

6. **You're NOT selling them. You're asking if they know anyone.** This removes the weirdness. If THEY express interest, they become an engaged lead.

### The Pitch Scripts

**Full version**: "By the way, do you know anybody who is [describe their struggles] looking to [dream outcome] in [time frame]? I'm taking on five case studies for free because that's all I can handle. I just want to get testimonials. I just had [name] work with me to get [dream outcome] even though she [same struggle]. I'd like to get more testimonials. Does anyone come to mind?"

**If they say no**: "Haha, does anyone you HATE come to mind?" (Often gets the real response)

**Short version**: "I help [type of customer] get [dream outcome] in [time period] without [effort and sacrifice] and I guarantee [risk reversal]."

## First 5 Clients Framework (10 Steps)

### Step 1: Build Your List
- Pull all email contacts
- Pull all social media followers you can DM
- Export all phone contacts
- Combine = way more leads than you think

### Step 2: Pick One Platform
Start with the one where you have the most contacts.

### Step 3: Personalize Your Message
30 seconds of looking at their profile. Reference something real.

### Step 4: Reach Out to 100 People Per Day
"It took 20 years to send the first DM, 2 minutes to send the second. Everything must be hard before it can be easy."

### Step 5: Warm Them Up (ACA Framework)
Acknowledge -> Compliment -> Ask. Ask if they know anyone, not selling them.

### Step 6: Offer It Free
"I'll do all this for free as long as you: (1) use my service, (2) give feedback, (3) leave a review if deserved."
You're getting the better end of the deal. You probably suck. Get reps. Lower barrier = more people.

### Step 7: Handle "Even Free Is Too Expensive"
If people won't work with you even for free, ask WHY. The reason = hidden costs you need to eliminate. Fix those = charge MORE later.

### Step 8: Start Back at the Top
Work through the list again. Some people will refer others = free leads. 3,000 outreaches with a free offer = guaranteed 5 takers.

### Step 9: Start Charging
When referrals start coming: free -> 80% off -> 60% off -> 40% off -> 20% off -> full price -> ABOVE full price. No rules on the ceiling.

### Step 10: Keep Them Warm
Check in regularly, provide value, post testimonials. Eventually they solicit YOU.

## Start Free, Then Raise Prices
- Make it free for first 5 clients. Terms of exchange: (1) actually use the service, (2) give feedback, (3) leave a killer review if deserved.
- Warren Buffett offered to work for Ben Graham for FREE. Graham said "you're overpriced" because training costs time.
- Once referrals start coming in, start charging: free -> 80% off -> 60% off -> 40% off -> 20% off -> full price -> ABOVE full price.
- Reaching capacity creates true scarcity. Then transition free clients: "I'm accepting a paying client for your spot. Would you like to match?"

## The "Ham and Garlic" Principle
- You have to give people what they WANT in order to give them what they NEED.
- Market the result, not the vehicle ("Big Booty Boot Camp" not "6-Week Deadlift Seminar").
- Meet people where they are. Market to their current truth, not your expert truth.
- Earn trust first, THEN deliver the real solution (garlic wrapped in ham).

## Warm Outreach Benchmarks
- Average 9 follow-up attempts needed
- Contact multiple times in multiple ways (phone, text, DM, email)
- Cindy might have been at Disney World, not ignoring you
- Wait 3-6 months, then work the list again (circumstances change)
- KEEP THEM WARM: Check in, provide value, post testimonials. Eventually they'll come to you.
